Using Mobile Control

The timer generates a local web-panel automatically. To control it from your phone:

  1. Connect both your Phone and Laptop to the SAME WiFi router (or mobile hotspot).
  2. In the timer app, look for the QR Code at the top right.
  3. Open your phone's camera and scan the QR code.
  4. A control panel will instantly open in your phone's browser!
